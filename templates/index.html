Quick Focus Tips

Even the best tracker is useless if your study habits are garbage. These small changes matter.

📵 Remove Easy Distractions

Keep your phone away from your study table. “I’ll just check one notification” is how 45 minutes disappear without you noticing.

🧠 Study One Subject Per Session

Jumping between subjects feels productive but often destroys deep focus. One session, one subject, one clear target.

⏰ Use Short Honest Sessions

If you can only focus for 30 minutes, track 30 minutes honestly. Fake 3-hour sessions help nobody.

📈 Review Your Pattern Weekly

Don’t just collect data. Check which days you focus best, which subjects drain you, and when distractions spike.
